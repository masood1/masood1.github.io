ABOUT

Bshah Tech India, head quartered in Mysuru, is a company with a focus on Executive Search, Recruitment, Training & Learning and Temporary Staffing services to client organisations.

Our Team :

Our team comprises qualified professionals who have strong rapport with Global Talent Pool and have experience in multiple disciplines including Human Resources, Technology, Sales and Marketing. Our team consists of highly ambitious knowledgeable and confident recruiters with great service attitude which is coupled with a desire to excel and innovate.

Client Relations : 

Client satisfaction is our prime concern, therefore we have our Client Relations Representatives who are also leading a team of recruiters & will be responsible to discover, respond to and resolve all Client issues quickly and professionally.

Our Vision

We inspire individuals and organisations to work more effectively and efficiently, and create greater choice in the domain of work, for the benefit of all concerned. As the world's leading provider of HR solutions – a business that has a positive impact on millions of people every day – we are conscious of our global role. Helping people to better work, better life is our common purpose and the way in which we contribute to society.

Confidentiality :

What is our profile ?

Bshah Tech was founded in 2010. BullehShah Technologies is based out of Bangalore & services clients across the country & abroad. Since our inception we have been in the business scenario with a mission of serving the companies with the speed and quality that is required. It is a dream relished by our team of professionals with varied experiences related to the service industry. Spurred on by the experience and expertise in talent search and rehabilitation of human resources, we have been providing the support to our esteemed clients to their satisfaction. We strongly believe that quality is a process not an end result. Therefore we have selected highly professional recruiters with strong technical background. . They are with BullehShah Technologies Services not only because of their experience and expertise, but also their desire to excel in the field of proper deployment of resources. Thus it is the result of their knowledge and rich experience, that we are able to find the right person at the right time and also at the right price.
